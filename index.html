Features

Everything You Need

Powerful analytics features designed for Discord community managers who want real insights, not just numbers.

📊

Server Statistics

Track total messages, member joins, leaves, and net growth. See your community's health at a glance with peak activity hours and top contributors.

📈

PDF Reports

Generate beautiful, branded PDF analytics reports with charts and insights. Choose from 7-day, 30-day, or custom date ranges.

📅

Scheduled Reports

Automate your analytics with daily, weekly, or monthly scheduled PDF reports delivered straight to a channel of your choice.

📊

Data Export

Export your analytics data as CSV or JSON for external analysis. Full control over your data with custom date range selection.

🔥

Activity Heatmaps

Visual heatmaps showing when your community is most active by day of week and hour. Find peak times and optimize your content schedule.

👥

Member Retention

Track member join/leave patterns and understand your retention rate. See who's staying and who's leaving.

🏆

User Streaks & Leaderboard

Track consecutive daily activity for your members. Reward your most engaged community members with a streak leaderboard.

🎤

Voice Analytics

Monitor voice channel usage, session duration, and participation. Understand how your community connects beyond text.

😀

Reaction Analytics

See the most-used emojis and reaction patterns across your server. Understand what resonates with your community.

🔔

Smart Alerts

Set custom thresholds for messages, joins, leaves, reactions, or voice activity. Get notified when metrics hit your targets.

🔍

Keyword Tracking

Configure keywords to monitor across your server. Track mentions, trends, and conversations that matter to you.

👻

Ghost Ping Detection

Never miss a mention. FD-DAB detects deleted and edited messages that mention you or your roles.

📋

Onboarding Tracking

Define onboarding steps for new members and track their progress through your server's onboarding flow.

🎯

Event Analytics

Track the impact of your events with before/during/after metrics. Measure engagement, joins, and activity changes.

🎨

Custom Branding

Brand your PDF reports with your server's logo, colors, and custom title. Make analytics truly yours.

🔗

Webhook Integrations

Push analytics data to external services via webhooks. Integrate with your existing tools and dashboards.

Commands

Simple & Powerful

Everything is accessible through intuitive Discord slash commands.

/analytics stats View server statistics — messages, joins, leaves, peak hours, top contributors, retention
/analytics reports generate Generate a 7-day PDF analytics report with charts and insights
/analytics reports monthly Generate a 30-day PDF analytics report
/analytics reports custom Generate a PDF report for a custom date range
/analytics reports csv Export analytics data as CSV for external analysis
/analytics reports json Export analytics data as JSON
/analytics heatmap View activity heatmap by day of week and hour
/analytics reactions Show most-used emojis and reaction statistics
/analytics voice Show voice channel usage stats and session data
/analytics roles Show role activity rankings
/analytics schedule Configure automated scheduled reports (daily/weekly/monthly)
/analytics streak Check your own activity streak
/analytics leaderboard View the activity streaks leaderboard
/analytics ghostpings Show recent ghost pings (deleted @mentions)
/analytics branding Customize PDF report branding (logo, colors, title)
/analytics webhooks Configure webhook integrations for external services
/analytics help View all available analytics commands
